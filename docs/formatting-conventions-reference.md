# Formatting Conventions Quick Reference

🌐 **Language**: English | [한국어](ko/formatting-conventions-reference.md)

> Cross-language formatting reference for the Legal Writing Agent.

## Korean vs. English Convention Comparison

| Feature | Korean (한국법) | English (US) | English (UK) | English (Intl) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Page size** | A4 | US Letter | A4 | A4 |
| **Body font** | 바탕체 12pt | Times New Roman 12pt | Times New Roman 12pt | Times New Roman 12pt |
| **Heading font** | 맑은 고딕 | Times New Roman/Arial | Arial | Arial |
| **Numbering** | 조·항·호·목 | Art/Sec/(a)/(i) | Clause/1.1/(a) | Art/Sec/Para/(a) |
| **Citation** | 「법률명」 제N조 | Bluebook | OSCOLA | Neutral |
| **Date** | YYYY년 MM월 DD일 | Month DD, YYYY | DD Month YYYY | DD Month YYYY |
| **Register** | 문어체 (~한다) | Formal (must/may) | Formal (must/may) | Neutral formal |
| **Margins** | 20/15/20/20mm | 1″ all | 1″ all | 25mm all |

## Register Quick Check

### Korean — Correct Endings
- ~한다, ~하여야 한다, ~할 수 있다, ~로 한다, ~에 해당한다
- Advisory: ~합니다 체 also acceptable

### English — Modal Verbs
- **Must** = obligation (preferred over "shall")
- **May** = permission
- **Will** = future action / declaration
- **Should** = recommendation (non-binding)
