# Template: General Legal (General Legal Documents) — English

## Use This Template For
- Policy documents
- Compliance guidelines
- Internal regulations
- Training materials
- Legal newsletters
- Translation or terminology guides

## Support Profile
- Support level: `Full`
- No authority packet is required for baseline drafting, though supporting sources should still be cited where the user expects them

## Section Tags
- `[B]` = Structural or administrative content
- `[S]` = Substantive explanatory, policy, or procedural content

## Boilerplate vs Substantive Guidance
- Boilerplate: titles, effective-date language, article numbering, review-cycle language, resource headings
- Substantive: definitions, requirements, procedures, examples, consequences, action items
- Choose a more explanatory tone for training or newsletter content and a more prescriptive tone for regulations or policies

## Drafting Reminders
- Keep section titles user-facing and functional; avoid labels like `Miscellaneous` unless genuinely necessary
- For policies and regulations, identify who must do what, when, and how compliance is checked
- For guides and training content, prefer examples and checklists over dense abstract exposition
