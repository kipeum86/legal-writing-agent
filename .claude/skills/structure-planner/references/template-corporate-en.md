# Template: Corporate (Corporate Documents) — English

## Board Resolution
```
1. Title: BOARD RESOLUTION OF [COMPANY NAME]
2. Recitals
   - WHEREAS, [background]...
3. Resolutions
   - RESOLVED, THAT [resolution 1]...
   - FURTHER RESOLVED, THAT [resolution 2]...
4. Certification
5. Signature Block
   Secretary: _____________
   Date: _____________
```

## Articles of Incorporation
```
ARTICLE I — NAME
ARTICLE II — PURPOSE
ARTICLE III — REGISTERED OFFICE AND AGENT
ARTICLE IV — AUTHORIZED SHARES
ARTICLE V — DIRECTORS
ARTICLE VI — INCORPORATOR
ARTICLE VII — INDEMNIFICATION
ARTICLE VIII — AMENDMENTS
```

## Meeting Minutes
```
1. Header: MINUTES OF [TYPE] MEETING OF [ENTITY]
2. Date, Time, Place
3. Attendance / Quorum
4. Call to Order
5. Agenda Items
   - Item 1: [Description]
   - Item 2: [Description]
6. Adjournment
7. Certification
8. Signature Block
```

## Notes
- Support level: Full
- US corporate conventions: WHEREAS/RESOLVED format for resolutions
- All caps for article headings in formal corporate documents
