# Template: Regulatory (Regulatory Documents) — English

## Use This Template For
- License or permit applications
- Regulatory compliance reports
- Government submissions or comment letters
- Regulatory inquiry responses

## Support Profile
- Support level: `Conditional`
- A complete submission requires the governing regulation, the agency's form or filing guidance if available, and a reliable factual basis
- If any of those are missing, maintain the structure and use placeholders rather than asserting compliance without support

## Section Tags
- `[B]` = Standard submission framework
- `[S]` = Applicant-specific or matter-specific content
- `[AP]` = Regulation, filing instruction, or authority packet required

## Variant A — License / Permit Application

1. `Application Title and Filing Identifier` `[B]`
2. `Applicant Information` `[S]`
   - Legal name
   - Address
   - Contact information
   - Registration or license number if applicable
3. `Application Type and Requested Action` `[S/AP]`
4. `Legal Basis for the Application` `[AP]`
5. `Operational / Factual Background` `[S]`
6. `Compliance with Regulatory Requirements` `[S/AP]`
   - `Requirement 1`
   - `Supporting facts`
   - `Supporting documents`
7. `Outstanding Issues and Remediation Plan` `[S]` where disclosure is required or strategically appropriate
8. `Attachments` `[B/S]`
9. `Certification / Signature` `[B/S]`

## Variant B — Regulatory Compliance Report

1. `Report Cover and Submission Information` `[B]`
2. `Purpose and Reporting Basis` `[S/AP]`
3. `Scope of Reviewed Operations` `[S]`
4. `Applicable Regulatory Framework` `[AP]`
5. `Compliance Status by Requirement` `[S/AP]`
6. `Deficiencies, Incidents, or Exceptions` `[S]`
7. `Corrective Actions and Timelines` `[S]`
8. `Management Attestation / Certification` `[B/S]`
9. `Appendices` `[B/S]`

## Variant C — Comment Letter / Government Submission

1. `Addressee and Submission Title` `[B]`
2. `Introduction and Interest of the Commenter` `[S]`
3. `Executive Summary of Requested Outcome` `[S]`
4. `Background and Regulatory Context` `[S/AP]`
5. `Detailed Comments` `[S/AP]`
   - `A. Issue heading`
   - `1. Current rule or proposal`
   - `2. Commenter's position`
   - `3. Supporting rationale and authorities`
   - `4. Proposed revision or requested action`
6. `Conclusion` `[S]`
7. `Signature Block` `[B/S]`

## Variant D — Regulatory Inquiry Response

1. `Question Presented` `[S]`
2. `Assumed Facts / Scope` `[S]`
3. `Applicable Regulation or Guidance` `[AP]`
4. `Response` `[S/AP]`
5. `Reservations / Limitations` `[B/S]`
6. `Attachments or Source Materials` `[B/S]`

## Boilerplate vs Substantive Guidance
- Boilerplate: cover information, filing identifiers, certification text, appendix labels, signature blocks
- Substantive: legal basis, compliance matrix, regulatory context, comment positions, corrective actions
- Conditional support warning: do not state that requirements are satisfied unless the supporting facts and regulatory basis are provided

## Drafting Reminders
- Agency forms and instructions override this template whenever they exist
- Use a requirement-by-requirement structure for applications and compliance reports whenever feasible
- Separate legal basis, factual support, and requested agency action so the submission is auditable
