# US English Document Page Setup

## US Letter Standard
| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Page size | US Letter (8.5″ × 11″ / 215.9mm × 279.4mm) |
| Top margin | 1″ (25.4mm) |
| Bottom margin | 1″ |
| Left margin | 1″ |
| Right margin | 1″ |

## Typography
| Element | Font | Size | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Body text | Times New Roman | 12pt | Regular |
| Heading 1 | Times New Roman or Arial | 14pt | Bold |
| Heading 2 | Times New Roman or Arial | 13pt | Bold |
| Heading 3 | Times New Roman or Arial | 12pt | Bold |
| Caption | Times New Roman | 10pt | Regular |
| Footnote | Times New Roman | 10pt | Regular |

## Line Spacing
| Document type | Spacing |
|---|---|
| Court briefs | Double-spaced |
| Legal memos | 1.5 spacing |
| Corporate documents | 1.15–1.5 spacing |

## Paragraph
- First line indent: 0.5″ (for briefs and memos)
- Paragraph spacing: 12pt after (or use double-spacing with no extra spacing)
- Alignment: Left-aligned (memos) or Justified (formal documents)
