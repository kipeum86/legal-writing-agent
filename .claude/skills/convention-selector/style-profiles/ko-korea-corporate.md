# Style Profile: ko-korea-corporate

Use this compact profile for Korean corporate drafting where the subtype is supported as Full, especially simple board resolutions and simple shareholders meeting minutes.

## Load Scope

- Load this file instead of the broad bilingual formatting guide.
- Do not load English, UK, international, or Mode A-D references unless the user requests cross-border or modal output.
- Apply a house style overlay only when a matching `/library/house-styles/` profile is selected.

## Core Conventions

- Language/register: formal Korean legal drafting. Resolutions and minutes use concise institutional prose.
- Numbering: title, meeting information block, agenda/resolution items, signature block. Use `제1호`, `제2호` or `1.`, `2.` consistently depending on template.
- Page setup: A4; body 12 pt; Korean corporate documents may use 바탕체 or the selected house style body font.
- Dates: `YYYY년 MM월 DD일`.
- Signatures: company/entity name, title, name, seal marker `(인)` where appropriate.

## Drafting Boundary

- Simple board resolutions and simple minutes may be drafted from meeting facts and agenda instructions.
- Articles, bylaws, internal regulations, powers of attorney, proxies, and organizational regulations are Conditional and require user-supplied clause instructions or an authority packet.
- Do not supply legal conclusions, risk assessments, or strategic recommendations.
