# Style Profile: en-us-advisory

Use this compact profile for US-jurisdiction English legal memoranda and advisory drafts.

## Load Scope

- Load this file instead of the broad bilingual formatting guide.
- Do not load UK, Korean, international, or Mode A-D references unless requested.

## Core Conventions

- Register: formal legal English, no contractions, concise issue-focused prose.
- Framework: IRAC/CREAC as selected by template.
- Citations: Bluebook-style citations for user-provided authorities; missing authority becomes `[Citation needed: ...]`.
- Page setup: US Letter, 1 inch margins, Times New Roman 12 pt unless house style overrides.

## Drafting Boundary

Advisory documents are Conditional. Do not independently create legal conclusions, risk assessments, recommendations, or certainty levels.
