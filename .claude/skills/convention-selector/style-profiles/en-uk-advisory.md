# Style Profile: en-uk-advisory

Use this compact profile for UK-jurisdiction English advisory memoranda.

## Load Scope

- Load this file only for UK English advisory work.
- Do not load US Bluebook, Korean, international, or Mode A-D references unless requested.

## Core Conventions

- Register: formal UK legal English.
- Citation: OSCOLA-style formatting for user-provided authorities.
- Numbering: clauses/sections follow UK-oriented template conventions.
- Page setup: A4, Times New Roman 12 pt unless house style overrides.

## Drafting Boundary

Advisory documents are Conditional. Unsupported legal conclusions, risk assessments, and recommendations must become counsel placeholders.
